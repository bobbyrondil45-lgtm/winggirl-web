The Model

Not a swipe app.
A curated ecosystem.

Wing Girl flips the traditional dating power structure. Women curate, vouch, and protect. Men earn their place through social proof.

01
👩

Wing Girl Nominates

A verified woman creates a profile for a man she trusts — her brother, friend, coworker. She writes his bio, green flags, dating intent, and uploads photos he approves.

02
🔗

Man Gets Invited

He receives an invite link. He verifies his identity, reviews his profile, approves photos and info — then he's live. He cannot join any other way.

03
💛

Women Browse & Like

Women daters scroll editorial-style profiles. Limited daily likes keep it intentional. No swiping. No volume games. Just curated discovery.

04
💬

He Initiates, She Decides

Men only see women who liked them. They must send the first message — encouraging effort and respect. Women receive zero unsolicited messages.

User Roles

Three roles.
One ecosystem.

Every person on Wing Girl plays a specific, meaningful role. No lurking. No low effort. No filler.

Wing Girl

The Curator

  • Women-only signup with ID + selfie verification
  • Create profiles for men you trust (friend, family, coworker)
  • Write bio, green flags, personality highlights, dating intent
  • Upload photos — man approves before going live
  • Earn badges: "Sister Approved," "Friend Vouched," "Cousin Certified"
  • Earn rewards for quality matches, not just volume
The Man

Invite-Only

  • Cannot sign up independently — invite link only
  • Must verify identity and approve all profile info
  • Cannot browse women freely
  • Only sees women who have liked him
  • Must initiate all conversations
  • Optional: add voice note "About Me"
Women Dater

The Chooser

  • Browse verified, vouched men only
  • Limited daily likes — intentionality over volume
  • Zero unsolicited messages ever
  • Filter by age, distance, intentions, values
  • Earn rewards: boosts, read receipts, exclusive profiles
  • Match + date confirmation = bonus points

Trust & Safety

Safer than Tinder.
By design.

Safety isn't a feature here — it's the foundation. The entire model is built around accountability and verified social proof.

🔒

Dual Verification

Wing Girls verify with ID + selfie. Men verify identity before activation. No anonymous profiles ever.

🛡️

Wing Girl Accountability

Reports are tied to both the man and his Wing Girl. Bad actors affect the person who vouched for them.

📍

Date Check-In

Built-in date check-in feature. Let a friend know you're safe — without leaving the app.

🚫

Soft Ban System

Repeated bad behavior triggers soft bans. Zero tolerance for harassment — consequences for the voucher too.

🏅

Trust Badges

"Sister Approved," "Friend Vouched," "Cousin Certified" — every man carries visible social proof.

🔕

No Unsolicited Messages

Women never receive messages from men who haven't been liked first. Period.
